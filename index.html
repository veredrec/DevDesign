ABOUT

Where the story begins

Born and raised in Jerusalem, Israel, I studied History and Focused on education and non-profit work in Israel and from 2011, in the USA.
I transitioned to Web Development in 2016, leaving a great job to pursue a dream of making a difference through technology. I started as a self-taught developer, acquiring knowledge from a wide variety of available online tools, mainly FreeCodeCamp and my dear friend, Google.
I continued in a 12-week Web Development Bootcamp, Founders and Coders, where I was also selected to lead and mentor the following cohort.
Based in the U.S (New Jersey, Indiana, and Maryland) I work as a UX Designer and UI Developer at the cloud and data science enterprise - Aunalytics.

What do I do?

As a User Experience Designer, I believe in user-centered design, in asking challenging questions, thinking of ways to make interfaces more intuitive, easier to use, and better designed.
As a User Interface Developer, I try to translate designs to user-facing platforms, built in clean, reusable code, while always aiming for best practices in my code.

What am I passionate about? (yes, that word again, but I really am!)

User centered design, but for real. Thinking about the users, understanding the uses needs and requirements, talking and learning from the users - by conducting interviews, observations, ideation sessions, surveys, etc.
That is what motivates me to create the best improvements, new features and designs I can.
